Exercise: Curls

Equipment: Barbell


Description:

Stand up straight, feet at shoulder width / Grip the barbell at shoulder width where palms are facing up / Bring the barbell to the starting position where arms are fully straight at the sides of the body / For the duration of the movement, keep shoulders retracted, chest up, and elbows close to the body / Take a deep breath / Keep elbows at a fixed position and curl the barbell up by bending the elbows, bringing the barbell to the front of mid chest / Lower the barbell in a controlled manner to the starting position by extending the arms, all while keeping elbow at a fixed position / As barbell is being lowered, breathe out
