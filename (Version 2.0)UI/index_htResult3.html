Exercise: Close Grip Push Ups

Equipment: n/a

Description:

  1. Lay flat on your stomach with all body parts close to the body
  2. Plant hands underneath the shoulders
  3. Extend the arms to lift yourself off the floor into the starting position
  4. Stay on your toes and hands
  5. should be pointed straight ahead
  6. For the duration of this movement, keep the back flat and abdominals tight
  7. Take a deep breath
  8. Bend arms to 90 degrees and keep the elbows back and close to the body at all times
  9. Press the floor away by extending the arms back into the starting position, all while breathing out
