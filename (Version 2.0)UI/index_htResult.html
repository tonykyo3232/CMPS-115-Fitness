How-To/Tutorials



Close Grip Bench Press

Equipment: Barbell

Description: Lay flat on bench / For the duration of movement, arch lower back slightly, squeeze shoulder blades back and down, and plant feet flat on the floor or stay on toes / Grip barbell in the racked position at shoulder width / Unrack barbell and bring to starting position directly above the center of the chest / Elbow should be facing 90 degrees away from the body at starting position / Take a deep breath / Hold breath and lower barbell to touch the center of chest in a linear motion / As barbell is descending, turn elbows in 45 degrees and tuck elbows towards the body / When barbell touches chest, press barbell upward in a linear motion back to the starting position / As barbel is ascending, breathe out and flare elbow 90 degrees away from the body
