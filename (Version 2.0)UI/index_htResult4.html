Exercise: Skull Crushers

Equipment: Barbell

Description:

  1. Lay flat on bench
  2. For the duration of movement, arch lower back slightly and plant feet flat on the floor
  3. Grip barbell in the racked position at shoulder width
  4. Unrack barbell and bring to starting position directly in front of the collarbone or upper chest
  5. Elbow should be facing 45 degrees away from the body at starting position
  6. Keep elbow at this fixed position for the duration of the movement
  7. Take a deep breath
  8. Hold breath and lower barbell to the top of the head
  9. Do this by bending at the elbows, but the elbows can not descend
  10. When barbell is at the top of the head, bring the barbell back to the starting position by extending the arms
  11. As arms are extending, breathe out
  12. Elbow is still fixed when extending the arms
