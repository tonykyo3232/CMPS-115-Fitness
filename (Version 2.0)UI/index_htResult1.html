Exercise: Curls

Equipment: Barbell

Description:

  1. Stand up straight, feet at shoulder width
  2. Grip the barbell at shoulder width where palms are facing up
  3. Bring the barbell to the starting position where arms are fully straight at the sides of the body
  4. For the duration of the movement, keep shoulders retracted, chest up, and elbows close to the body
  5. Take a deep breath
  6. Keep elbows at a fixed position and curl the barbell up by bending the elbows, bringing the barbell to the front of mid chest
  7. Lower the barbell in a controlled manner to the starting position by extending the arms, all while keeping elbow at a fixed position
  8. As barbell is being lowered, breathe out
